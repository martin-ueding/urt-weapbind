############
urt-weapbind
############

In the game Urban Terror, changing your weapon and gear selection can be done
through the menu. This takes some time, and you cannot *quickly* change your
weapon set. There is a possibility to generate a binding, which is not easy by
hand.

This script will take your selection and generate a binding for you.
